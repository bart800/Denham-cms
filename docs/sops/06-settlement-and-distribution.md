## 1. Overview

The settlement process covers everything from accepting a settlement offer through distributing funds to the client. Key steps: receive settlement docs ‚Üí contact client ‚Üí get signatures/notarization ‚Üí deposit check ‚Üí wait 10 business days ‚Üí calculate distribution ‚Üí distribute funds ‚Üí close file.

---

## 2. Settlement Offer Process

When presenting a settlement offer to Opposing Counsel:

1. **Get authorization to settle** from the client
2. **Attach the firm's W9**
3. **Request check be issued to:**

**Preferred format:**
> Bart Denham Law PLLC F.B.O. [Insured Name(s)], [PA Name if applicable], [Mortgage Company if applicable]

**Alternative (split check):**
> ¬Ω to Bart Denham Law PLLC F.B.O. [Insured Name(s)], [PA Name]
> ¬Ω to the Mortgage Company and Client

4. Confirm with the client that they have the same mortgage company on file

## 3. Receiving the Settlement Packet

1. Create a **"Settlement"** folder in the client's file
2. Download the settlement packet
3. Open in Adobe Acrobat:
   - **All tools** ‚Üí **Scan & OCR** ‚Üí **Recognize Text** ‚Üí In this file (makes PDF searchable)
   - **Edit** ‚Üí **Organize Pages** ‚Üí Extract pages as separate files
4. Split into separate PDFs:
   - ‚úÖ Settlement Letter
   - ‚úÖ Check
   - ‚úÖ Agreed Order of Dismissal (AOD)
   - ‚úÖ Settlement Agreement and Release
5. Rename files and save to the Settlement folder
6. **Log the settlement amount in the CMS**

### 4.1 Call the Client

**If they answer:**

> Hello, this is [your name] from Denham Property & Injury Law Firm. We received the settlement amount of [amount]. To distribute the settlement, you need to come into the office to endorse the check.

## 5. In-Person vs. Remote Notarization

### In-Person
- Client brings the Settlement Agreement and Release to a notary
- Client comes to the office to endorse the check and deliver notarized documents

### Remote (Online Notary)
- Use **onlinenotary.us**
- Set up the appointment:
  - Add the client's email for the video call link
  - Have the finalized document and bill come to our office (use your email)
  - Pay the bill and download documents when received
- Client needs a webcam

## 7. Settlement Check Handling

### 7.1 Receiving the Check

1. **Scan** front and back of the settlement check immediately upon receipt
2. **Save** scanned image to:
   - CMS ‚Üí Negotiations section
   - OneDrive backup folder
   - **Naming format:** `[Date of check] - [Payor] - [Amount] - [Client Name]`
3. **Deliver** physical check to Office Manager
4. Office Manager logs the check details in the tracking spreadsheet

### 7.2 Endorsement Rules

| Check Says | Action Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| Written **FBO** | No POA needed ‚Äî endorsement by client only |
| Does NOT say FBO | POA needed, OR client must come in to endorse |
| Multiple parties listed | All parties must sign |

### 7.3 Deposit & Waiting Period

1. Bart deposits checks at the bank
2. Office Manager notes the date of deposit
3. ‚ö†Ô∏è **Clients must wait 10 business days after deposit** before any funds are disbursed
4. During the waiting period, staff can prepare distribution calculations

## 8. Distribution Statement Preparation

### 8.1 Check the Contract for Fee Percentages

| Scenario | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| Pre-suit settlement (no complaint filed) | Pre-litigation percentage |
| Post-filing settlement (complaint filed) | Litigation percentage |

Look in the client's file to determine if a complaint was filed.

### 8.2 Create Distribution Spreadsheet

Include:
- **Total settlement amount**
- **Attorney's fees** (per contract percentage)
- **Costs / Expenses:**
  - Filing fee (always if complaint filed, usually ~$300)
  - Mailing fees
  - Expert fees
- **Public Adjuster fees** (check PA contract ‚Äî cannot exceed 10%)
  - ‚ö†Ô∏è PA is the only other party we distribute fees to besides the client
- **Net to client**

### 8.3 Review Process
1. Email the distribution spreadsheet to Bart for review
2. Once approved, email the settlement breakdown to the client
3. Ask for POA if needed
4. Request a Google review

### 8.4 Log in CMS
- Go to the project ‚Üí Expenses section ‚Üí New Award
- Enter all settlement data

## 10. Release Return & AOD Filing

1. Have the client execute the release
2. Send the executed release to Opposing Counsel
3. Sign the Agreed Order of Dismissal (AOD) and return to OC
4. Confirm the AOD is filed and entered with the court
5. Confirm all checks clear

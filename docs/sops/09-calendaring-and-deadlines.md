## 3. Date Calculation Rules

### The Rule for Litigation Documents

> **Always start counting on the next business day** from the date you serve or receive a document. **Include weekends** in the count (unless the deadline falls on a weekend/holiday, in which case it extends to the next business day).

### Examples

**Complaint served on 10/07/25:**
- Day 1 = 10/08/25 (next business day)
- 20th day = 10/27/25 (answer due)

**Demand sent on 9/18/24:**
- Count 14 days including weekends
- Deadline = 10/02/24

### Demand Deadlines (Non-Litigation)
- Weekends **DO** count
- Start counting from the day sent

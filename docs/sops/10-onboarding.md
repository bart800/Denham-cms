## 1. Overview

This SOP covers onboarding a new employee from pre-arrival through their first month. The goal is to get them set up with all required tools, familiar with firm procedures, and productive as quickly as possible.

**Onboarding is everyone's responsibility** ‚Äî the Office Manager leads the process, but attorneys and paralegals should be available to answer questions and provide guidance during the new hire's first weeks.

## 8. OneDrive & File Access

### Accept Shared Folder Invitations

1. Check email for shared OneDrive folder invitations
2. Click invitation link and sign in
3. Accept shared folder invitations for: **"Clio"** and **"Back Up"**

### Add OneDrive Shortcuts (Windows)

1. Open File Explorer
2. Sign into OneDrive account
3. Open OneDrive in browser ‚Üí navigate to **Shared** section
4. Locate shared folders ("Clio" and "Back Up")
5. Click folder ‚Üí select **"Add shortcut to OneDrive"**
6. Folders will appear in File Explorer for easy access

### Add OneDrive Shortcuts (Mac)

1. Open Finder
2. Sign into OneDrive via the desktop app (download from Mac App Store if needed)
3. In browser, go to onedrive.live.com ‚Üí sign in ‚Üí click **"Shared"**
4. Locate shared folders ("Clio" and "Back Up")
5. Click each folder ‚Üí select **"Add shortcut to My files"**
6. Folders will sync and appear in Finder under OneDrive

## 14. Common Mistakes & Pro Tips

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes New Hires Make

| Mistake | Why It's Bad | Prevention |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| **Not reading the SOPs** | Asks questions that are already documented | Read assigned SOPs on schedule; reference them before asking |
| **Saving files to the wrong location** | Documents get lost; team can't find them | Always save to CMS AND OneDrive backup ‚Äî follow naming conventions |
| **Forgetting to log communications** | No one knows what was said to the client | Log every call, email, and voicemail in CMS Activity section |
| **Giving legal advice** | Unauthorized practice of law | Only attorneys give legal advice ‚Äî you provide information and updates |
| **Not calendaring immediately** | Deadline gets forgotten | Calendar the moment you learn about a deadline ‚Äî don't wait |
| **Using personal email for work** | Security risk; no firm records | Always use @denham.law email |
| **Not asking questions** | Mistakes compound silently | Ask early and often ‚Äî it's expected during onboarding |
| **Trying to fix errors alone** | Small errors become big problems | Escalate immediately ‚Äî especially for deadlines and client funds |

### üí° Pro Tips for Getting Up to Speed Fast

- **Take notes** ‚Äî you'll get a lot of information in Week 1; write it down
- **Bookmark this SOP** ‚Äî you'll reference it repeatedly
- **Shadow before doing** ‚Äî watch a paralegal do the task before attempting it yourself
- **Use the SOP as a checklist** ‚Äî literally check off each step as you do it
- **Ask "why"** ‚Äî understanding the reason behind a procedure helps you remember it
- **Set up your CMS dashboard** ‚Äî customize it to show your assigned matters and tasks
- **Learn the naming conventions** early ‚Äî consistent file names save everyone time
- **Practice in the CMS** ‚Äî there's no substitute for hands-on experience
- **Keep a "questions" document** ‚Äî write down questions as they come up and batch them for your mentor
- **Don't be afraid to say "I don't know yet"** ‚Äî it's better than guessing wrong
